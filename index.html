What to Do When You Get a Dog

Prepare your home with a safe space, comfy bed, and essential supplies like food and toys. Your first vet visit is key to ensure your new dog is healthy. Spend quality time bonding and get your dog used to family members or other pets.

Training

Teach your dog basic commands like sit, stay, and come. Use positive reinforcement for successful training!

Health

Regular vet visits, vaccinations, and parasite control (e.g., flea, tick, heartworm) are essential for keeping your dog healthy. Look for signs of illness and take action early!
